Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These delicious cookies are a great option for non-pie lovers on this holiday of gratitude and food.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup of canned pumpkin
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp of ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp of ground cloves
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat butter until smooth
  2. Add sugars and beat until fluffy
  3. Add eggs, vanilla, and pumpkin
  4. In a large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg and cloves
  5. Add to wet mixture slowly
  6. Stir in chips
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until cookies are browned around the edges
