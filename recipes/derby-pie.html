Derby Pie

Description

This pie adds a non-fruit pie to the dessert table. Dessert that originated in Kentucky, where my mother's family is from.

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • 1 melted stick butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cupt of scaut flour
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 1 cup of pecans
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Mix together the ingredients
  2. Pour into unbaked pie shell at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
  3. Shake-if set in center-ready or cracked near edge
