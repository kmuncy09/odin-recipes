Apple Pie

Description

This apple pie is the perfect pie for Thanksgiving. This is my great-grandmother's recipe. It has past down through generations. It wasn't written down until my mother wrote it down.

Ingredients

  • 9" pie crust

    • For Filling:

  • 7-8 peeled and sliced Granny Smith apples
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3 tbsp of flour
  • 3 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of nutmeg

    • For Topping:

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 stick of butter, sliced in pats

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Place 9" pie crust, botton floured, in a pan. Cover the edges with foil.

    3. For Filling:

  3. Mix apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a large bowl until coated (the apples will look wet)
  4. Pour mixture into crust

    5. For Topping:

  5. Stir flour and sugar in a small bowl
  6. Cut/mash butter into mix with fork
  7. Pour over apples
  8. Place a pie pan over cookie sheet to catch loose topping
  9. Bake for about an hour, until golden brown and crispy with juices bubbling out
