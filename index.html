Thanksgiving Dessert Recipes

Thanksgiving Day features parades, a dog show, and most notably, food. A Thanksgiving feast features side dishes, desserts, and the glorious turkey centerpiece. Side dishes include corn cassarole, sweet potatoes, mashed potatoes, cranberry sauce, and rolls. Desserts often include a number of pies. From Apple Pie to cherry pie to pumpkin pie, or local favorites like Derby Pie or Boston Cream Pie, there are a number of options. But what if you don't like pie? Cookies, such as Pumpkin Chocolate Chip, or cakes, such as Pumpkin Angel Cake might be the perfect fit. Regardless of your preferences, there is something for you at Thanksgiving dinner.
